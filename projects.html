Los Carpinteros

There are thousands of good developers in Latin America who cannot speak english, and millions of clients in the developed world looking for affordable labor. This idea aims to bridge that gap, while providing a safety net for the customer that the job will always be ready. Because most people don't need a "rockstar" coder, they just need a reliable code carpenter.
